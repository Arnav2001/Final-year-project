XROS FELLOWSHIP

The XROS Fellowship is a three-month program designed to help young entrepreneurs turn their ideas into reality.

For more information, visit xrosfellowship.ficci.in.

GSHOCK

GSHOCK is a hackathon focused on building innovative solutions for the gaming industry.

For more information, visit gshockhackathon.com.

MLH

MLH is the official student hackathon league, with events held all over the world.

For more information, visit mlh.io.

HECTOBER FEST

Hectoberfest is a month-long celebration of open source software, with various events and activities organized by GitHub.

For more information, visit github.com/blog/2466-celebrate-open-source-this-october-with-hacktoberfest.
